AI Fiduciary Governance Certification™

Verifying Governance Authority, Not Technology

What Certification Means

Purpose

AI Fiduciary Governance Certification™ exists to verify governance authority, not to evaluate AI technology.

The certification confirms that an organization's use of AI operates within documented human fiduciary authority, with enforceable controls over publication, eligibility, and revocation.

What This Certification Confirms

A certified entity has demonstrated that:

  • AI is used only in defined, disclosed contexts
  • Human authority is explicitly documented and retained
  • Unauthorized publication or representation is prevented by system controls
  • Certification status is publicly verifiable
  • Certification can be revoked at any time with immediate effect
Certification status reflects governance readiness, not outcomes.

What This Certification Does NOT Confirm

This certification does not:

  • Approve or endorse AI models, tools, or vendors
  • Certify technical safety, accuracy, or performance
  • Guarantee legal, regulatory, or insurance outcomes
  • Replace regulatory approval, audits, or compliance programs
No claims of "compliance," "safety," or "approval" are made.

How Public Verification Works

Certification status is published in a read-only public registry.

Each entry includes:

  • Certification ID
  • Legal entity name
  • Jurisdiction
  • Status (Active / Expired / Revoked)
  • Issuance and expiration dates
  • High-level scope of certified AI use
Verification is performed by Certification ID only.

Public Registry

Registry Endpoint:

https://ttony106-source.github.io/afgc-registry/afgc-registry.json

This registry is:

  • Machine-readable (JSON)
  • Updated automatically
  • Limited to eligible, approved certifications only
Entries are removed immediately upon revocation or expiration.

Certification Status Definitions

Active Certification is valid and publicly recognized.

Expired Certification term has ended and is no longer valid.

Revoked Certification has been withdrawn with immediate effect.

Revocation overrides publication state and does not require explanation.

Revocation Authority

Certification may be revoked if:

  • Human authority changes without disclosure
  • Certified scope expands beyond what was issued
  • Override or governance controls are impaired
  • Material misrepresentation is identified
Revocation is unilateral and immediate.

Intended Audience

This certification is designed for:

  • Boards and fiduciaries
  • Insurers and underwriters
  • Development Finance Institutions (DFIs)
  • Regulators and examiners
  • Institutional partners
It is not a marketing badge or consumer trust seal.

Use of Certification

Certified entities may:

  • Reference certification status by ID
  • Link to the public registry entry

Certified entities may not:

  • Claim compliance, approval, or safety
  • Modify scope language
  • Represent certification as regulatory endorsement
Misuse may result in revocation.
